## Project Summary
A Python-based econometrics research project forecasting U.S. inflation using macroeconomic
time series data from the FRED API. The goal is to build, evaluate, and compare ARIMA and VAR
forecasting models, present findings in a Streamlit dashboard, and document the work in a
research paper.

**Repo:** https://github.com/ACKibler/inflation-forecast
**Dashboard:** https://inflation-forecast-vibe.streamlit.app/
**Owner:** Ashton Kibler
**Started:** 2026-03-31

## Data
All data from FRED API, January 2000 – February 2026, resampled to monthly frequency.
Saved to `data/macro_data.csv` (~314 rows × 5 columns). Gitignored — run `fetch_data.py` to regenerate.

| Variable | FRED ID | Description |
|---|---|---|
| CPI | CPIAUCSL | Consumer Price Index |
| UNRATE | UNRATE | Unemployment Rate |
| M2 | M2SL | M2 Money Supply |
| OIL | DCOILWTICO | WTI Crude Oil Price |
| FEDFUNDS | FEDFUNDS | Federal Funds Rate |

**Known data issue:** October 2025 missing for CPI and UNRATE on FRED — accepted gap, not a bug.
`fetch_data.py` uses `dropna(subset=["CPI","UNRATE"])`. Gap filled via `interpolate(method="time")` before modeling.

## Stationarity Findings
ADF tests in `src/stationarity.py`. All series stationary after treatment:

| Series | Treatment for modeling |
|---|---|
| CPI | Log-difference → DCPI (≈ monthly inflation rate) |
| UNRATE | First-difference → DUNRATE |
| M2 | Log-difference → DM2 |
| OIL | Levels (stationary) |
| FEDFUNDS | Levels (stationary; ZLB footnote in paper §3.1) |

## Model Results (verified by src/paper_analysis.py)

**Train:** Jan 2000 – Nov 2020 (250 obs) | **Test:** Dec 2020 – Feb 2026 (63 obs)

| Model | RMSE | MAE | MAPE |
|---|---|---|---|
| Random Walk | 43.78 | 39.64 | 12.79% |
| ARIMA(1,1,4) | 33.74 | 30.57 | 9.88% |
| VAR(4) | 25.64 | 23.36 | 7.55% |

**ARIMA:** AIC = −2251.70, BIC = −2230.57. Ljung-Box p ≈ 1.0 at lags 10 and 20. CUSUM test detects parameter instability (boundary breached at 2020 COVID break — max |CUSUM| = 5.17 vs bound = 4.86).

**VAR:** AIC selects lag 4, BIC selects lag 2. Durbin-Watson 1.94–2.04 across all equations.

**Granger causality (all Granger-cause DCPI at 5%):**

| Variable | F-stat | p-value |
|---|---|---|
| OIL | 20.90 | < 0.001 |
| FEDFUNDS | 5.11 | < 0.001 |
| DUNRATE | 3.28 | 0.011 |
| DM2 | 2.54 | 0.039 |
